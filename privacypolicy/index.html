Your Data Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) you have the right to obtain a portable copy of your data in both a human and machine-readable format. You also have the right to request that your data no longer be processed. Finally, you have the right to request that all data gathered about you be deleted at your request. To perform any of these actions, please send an email to Carson_Shook@outlook.com with one or more of the following headings:

  • Data Copy Request for carsonshook.com
  • Cease Data Processing Request for carsonshook.com
  • Data Deletion Request for carsonshook.com

In order to fulfill your request, please include your name or relevant username, as well as any other information you think is relevant to your request. In the event that a Data Deletion Request is made, your email data will be deleted from the owner's inbox following final correspondence notifying that your data has been deleted. Please allow up to one week to process any request, and if you do not receive a reply by the 8th day, please check that the address you are sending to is valid, and make note that this is your second attempt to contact. The owner and the website will do their best to work in good faith with each request.
